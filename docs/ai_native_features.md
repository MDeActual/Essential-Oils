
# AI-Native Features — Phyto.ai

Goal
Create an AI-native health discovery platform rather than a simple wellness tracker.

Feature 1 — Health Pattern Detector
AI identifies patterns between symptoms, protocols, and outcomes.

Example:
Users reporting night sweats respond better to cooling protocols.

Feature 2 — Protocol Personalization Engine
AI recommends personalized protocol variants based on:

• symptom cluster
• remedy inventory
• contributor archetype
• protocol performance history

Feature 3 — Discovery Feed Generator
AI summarizes discoveries for contributors.

Example Output:
Lavender + Magnesium improved sleep quality for 63% of participants.

Feature 4 — Contributor Archetype Detection
AI clusters users into response archetypes.

Examples
• Magnesium responders
• Aromatic responders
• Cooling responders
• Lifestyle responders

Feature 5 — Protocol Evolution Engine
AI detects high-performing protocol variants and suggests new versions.

Example
sleep_reset_v1 → sleep_reset_v2

Feature 6 — AI Insight Cards
Generated insights appear in the discovery feed to explain emerging patterns.

Feature 7 — Research Insight Generator
AI prepares anonymized research summaries from aggregated data.

All AI models train on anonymized outcome statistics only.
