# Pricing Model — Phyto.ai

Purpose:
Define who pays what, when, and what they get access to.

The platform serves three main audiences:
- contributors / users
- practitioners
- research / institutional buyers

---

# 1. Contributor / User Pricing

## Free Tier
Purpose:
Maximize participation and dataset growth.

Includes:
- join challenges
- receive protocols
- log outcomes
- basic discovery feed
- contributor profile
- basic social participation
- points and badges

## Premium Tier
Suggested range:
$8–$15 / month

Includes:
- personalized protocol recommendations
- advanced AI insights
- personal pattern analysis
- deeper discovery explorer access
- early access to challenges
- premium progress analytics
- advanced comparison tools
- priority support

---

# 2. Practitioner Pricing

## Practitioner Pro
Suggested range:
$39–$79 / month

Includes:
- verified practitioner badge
- practitioner Q&A participation
- practitioner directory listing
- practitioner analytics dashboard
- protocol outcome trend views
- lead generation opportunities
- ability to contribute structured protocol commentary

Access should depend on:
- verification
- reputation standards
- anti-spam rules

---

# 3. Research / Institutional Pricing

## Static Reports
Suggested range:
$2,000–$10,000 per report

Use cases:
- symptom-category outcome summaries
- protocol family analysis
- regional / geospatial summaries
- adherence and outcome trends

## Interactive Data Explorer
Suggested range:
$499–$1,999 / month

Includes:
- filter by symptom
- protocol family
- remedy class
- region
- archetype
- adherence
- time period

## Custom Research Projects
Suggested range:
$10,000–$100,000+

Use cases:
- sponsored protocol comparisons
- custom population analyses
- special reporting
- targeted discovery studies

---

# 4. Reward Offsets / Incentives

Points can unlock:
- bundle discounts
- free shipping
- premium months
- early access
- special badges

Example:
- 500 points → 10% bundle discount
- 1000 points → 20% bundle discount
- 2500 points → 1 month premium
- 5000 points → 3 months premium

---

# 5. Revenue Principles

- Do not paywall core participation too early.
- Charge for intelligence, convenience, and professional access.
- Keep community contribution free.
- Keep trust higher than monetization pressure.
- Never sell personal identities.
- Only monetise anonymized aggregated insights where appropriate.

---

# 6. Upgrade Timing

Contributors should encounter premium after they have already received value.

Suggested moments:
- after completing first challenge
- after seeing first personal insight
- after using discovery explorer
- after earning enough points to understand the reward system

---

# 7. Business Logic Summary

Free users grow the dataset.
Premium users fund the AI layer.
Practitioners fund the professional layer.
Researchers fund the data / insight layer.
