# Vision
Phyto.ai is a community-driven natural health discovery platform designed to determine which natural remedies actually work in real-world conditions using contributor data, structured protocols, ontology-driven reasoning, AI pattern detection, protocol intelligence, challenge-based engagement, geospatial analysis, and strong privacy protections.

The system combines:
- community data collection
- protocol experimentation
- challenge-based engagement
- AI pattern detection
- natural remedy knowledge graph
- protocol intelligence engine
- contributor archetypes
- personalized wellness insights
- synthetic simulation infrastructure
- reputation and integrity controls
- geospatial analytics
- structured social participation
- clear pricing architecture

# Reputation System
Contributors, practitioners, and community participants receive reputation scores based on:
- adherence
- completion
- reporting quality
- participation consistency
- helpfulness
- anomaly / trust signals

Reputation affects:
- weighting of data
- contributor recognition
- access to advanced opportunities
- professional trust levels

# Geospatial Data Layer
Location is opt-in and privacy-preserving.

The platform may collect approximate location, convert it to a coarse region or grid cell, discard raw coordinates, and use anonymized regional data for:
- protocol response mapping
- regional comparison
- climate / environmental pattern analysis
- founder dashboard mapping
- research summaries

---

# Social Layer
Structured social interaction exists through:
- challenge rooms
- protocol Q&A
- discovery discussions

The system is moderated and reputation-aware.
